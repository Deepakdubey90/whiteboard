Spark Your Classroom Collaboration

Studies have shown that students learn the best when they collaborate. Engage students into collaborative work and achieve better results. Students can collaborate with each other by simply sharing their board to their classmates.

Flourish Lessons Engagement

Redefine how students learn by letting them interact with lessons in both visual and kinesthetic way. Upload files into the board, share the board with your students and let them take notes or draw from a touch on their tablet screens.

Visualize Teaching in 3 Easy Steps

Project lessons to the whole class. Open AWW board on tablet, reopen the board on the PC connected to the projector and share the board with students via QR code. Everything you or your students draw will show on a projection screen.

Competitive pricing

With AWW every school can utilize advantages of a smart board without the need of buying one. You can get 42 AWW personal licences for a price of one standard smart board. See our pricing plans or contact us for custom quote.

No installation, no expensive hardware

No need for installation or buying expensive equipment. AWW works on any modern browser or device such as desktop computer, tablet or smartphone.
